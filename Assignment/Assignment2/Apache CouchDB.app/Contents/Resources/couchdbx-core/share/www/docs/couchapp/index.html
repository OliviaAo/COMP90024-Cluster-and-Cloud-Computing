6. CouchAppÂ¶

CouchApps are web applications served directly from CouchDB, mostly driven by JavaScript and HTML5. If you can fit your application into those constraints, then you get CouchDB’s scalability and flexibility “for free” (and deploying your app is as simple as replicating it to the production server).
